Privacy Policy

Ruth Devotional App  ·  Effective: March 2026  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Ruth is a personal devotional app. Your reflections are private and sacred. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how it is protected.

· · ·

What we collect

  • Account information — your name and email address, collected when you create an account.
  • Journal entries — the reflections you write during your daily devotion. These are stored securely in your account so Ruth can remember your journey across sessions.
  • Conversation history — your exchanges with Ruth, stored as part of your journal entry.
  • Mood and situation selections — used to personalize your verse and devotional content.
  • App usage data — streak counts, notification preferences, and subscription status.

What we do not collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your journal entries for advertising.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.

How your data is used

  • To provide the service — your journal content is sent to Anthropic's API (Claude) to generate Ruth's responses. This is the core function of the app.
  • To personalize your experience — Ruth builds a private memory of your journey so she can respond to you more personally over time.
  • To send reminders — if you enable daily reminders, we schedule a local notification on your device. No data leaves your device for this.

Third-party services

Ruth uses the following services to operate:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes your journal text and generates Ruth's responses. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — securely stores your account, journal entries, and profile data. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — manages subscription billing and purchase restoration. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple — processes payments for Ruth+ subscriptions via Apple's App Store. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with encryption at rest via Supabase. Your journal entries are associated with your account and are not accessible to other users. Claude API calls are proxied through a secure server — your Anthropic API key is never stored on your device.

Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the Settings screen. Deletion is permanent and removes all journal entries, conversation history, and account information from our servers within 30 days.

Children's privacy

Ruth is intended for users 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To export your data or request deletion outside the app, contact us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be communicated in the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
